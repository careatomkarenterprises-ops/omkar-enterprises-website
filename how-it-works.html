How Our Private Lending Partnership Works

A transparent, structured approach to generating predictable returns with contractual security

The Omkar Enterprises Process

Three simple steps from initial consultation to monthly returns

1

Step 1: Consultation & Capital Commitment

We begin with a confidential consultation to understand your investment goals and explain our structure in detail. You select your investment amount (₹25,000 to ₹25,00,000) and review the comprehensive loan agreement.

Key Activities:

  • Confidential consultation (30-60 minutes)
  • Review of investment tiers and terms
  • Detailed Q&A about the agreement
  • Selection of investment amount
2

Step 2: Agreement Execution & Funding

Once you're comfortable with the terms, we execute the formal Loan Agreement. You transfer funds via secure bank transfer, and we provide post-dated cheques as security. The agreement includes all payment schedules and security mechanisms.

Key Activities:

  • Signing of comprehensive Loan Agreement
  • Secure fund transfer via NEFT/RTGS
  • Provision of post-dated cheques as security
  • Welcome kit with payment schedule
3

Step 3: Monthly Returns & Principal Repayment

You receive fixed monthly returns of 2.5% via NEFT/IMPS, with the possibility of additional performance-based returns. After 12 months of interest payments, your principal is returned in the 13th month via bullet payment.

Key Activities:

  • Monthly returns by 10th working day
  • Optional performance-based returns
  • Monthly payment statements
  • Principal repayment in month 13
  • Option to renew or exit

Security & Protection Mechanisms

Multiple layers of protection for your investment

Comprehensive Agreement

10-page legally binding Loan Agreement detailing all terms, obligations, and dispute resolution mechanisms.

Post-Dated Cheques

Enforceable security instruments that become actionable after 90+ days of continuous non-payment.

Arbitration Clause

Mandatory arbitration in Pune for dispute resolution, ensuring faster settlement than court proceedings.

Limited Liability

Claims only against company assets, with clear separation from personal assets of directors.
