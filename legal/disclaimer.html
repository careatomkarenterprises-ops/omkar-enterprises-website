Risk Disclaimer

Understanding the risks before entering a private lending partnership

Critical Warning

Omkar Enterprises enters into private loan agreements with individuals. This is NOT a bank deposit, NOT insured, NOT regulated by RBI/SEBI as a deposit-taking entity, and NOT a guaranteed return scheme.

Capital is at risk. Returns depend on business performance. Past returns don't indicate future results.

Consult your financial and legal advisors before proceeding.

Comprehensive Risk Disclosure

Understanding all potential risks before investing

Capital Risk

HIGH RISK

Your entire principal investment is at risk. While secured by post-dated cheques and contractual obligations, there is no guarantee of full repayment.

Potential Scenarios:

Severe Loss

Complete loss of principal if Omkar Enterprises fails and has no recoverable assets.

Partial Loss

Partial recovery through legal proceedings may result in significant loss of principal.

Delayed Recovery

Full recovery possible but delayed through lengthy legal proceedings.

Liquidity Risk

MEDIUM RISK

Your funds are locked in for minimum 8 months. Early withdrawal is subject to penalties and recalculation.

Lock-in Period: 8 Months

No withdrawal permitted during this period except through mutual written consent.

Early Withdrawal Penalty

Returns recalculated at 1.5% monthly instead of 2.5% for actual period.

Processing Time

Early withdrawal requests processed within 90 working days.

Market & Business Risk

HIGH RISK

Returns depend on Omkar Enterprises' investment performance. While fixed returns are contractual, they rely on successful business operations.

Key Risk Factors:

Business Failure
Market Downturns
Operational Issues
Regulatory Changes
Management Errors
Economic Conditions

Security & Enforcement Risk

MEDIUM RISK

While post-dated cheques provide legal recourse, enforcement may involve time and costs.

Enforcement Process Timeline:

1
90-Day Grace Period

Cannot take action until 90 days of continuous non-payment

2
Legal Proceedings

Initiate legal action under Negotiable Instruments Act

3
Court Process

Legal proceedings can take 1-3 years minimum

4
Asset Recovery

Recovery depends on available assets

Potential Costs:

  • Legal fees and court costs
  • Time investment for proceedings
  • Recovery may be partial after costs

Regulatory Risk

LOW RISK

This is a private lending arrangement, not a regulated financial product.

No RBI Regulation

Not an NBFC activity requiring RBI registration

No SEBI Regulation

Not a collective investment scheme under SEBI

No Deposit Insurance

No DICGC insurance like bank deposits

Risk: Future regulatory changes could impact the structure or legality of such arrangements.

Contractual Risk

MEDIUM RISK

The Loan Agreement contains terms that may limit your rights in certain scenarios.

Limited Liability

Claims only against company assets, not personal assets of directors

Grace Period

90-day grace period for all payments before default can be declared

Arbitration Only

Dispute resolution only through arbitration in Pune

Return Recalculation

Early withdrawal recalculates returns at lower rate (1.5% instead of 2.5%)
