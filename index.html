Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our private lending partnerships

Is the 2.5% monthly return guaranteed?

It is contractually obligated under the Loan Agreement, not "guaranteed" in the regulatory sense. We are legally bound to pay it as per the agreed terms.

What security do I have for my investment?

You receive post-dated cheques as security, which become enforceable after 90+ days of continuous non-payment. Combined with the arbitration clause and our business reputation, this creates multiple layers of protection.

Can I exit before 13 months?

Yes, after the 8-month lock-in period. Returns are recalculated at 1.5% monthly for the actual period, with any excess treated as partial principal repayment as per Clause 2.4.
