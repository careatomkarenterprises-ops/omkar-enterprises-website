Onboarding Process

Your step-by-step journey to becoming a lending partner with Omkar Enterprises

5-Step Partnership Journey

From initial consultation to receiving your first monthly return

01

Initial Consultation

Days 1-3

We begin with a confidential consultation to understand your investment goals, explain our structure in detail, and answer all your questions.

What to Expect:

  • Video/Phone Call: 30-60 minute confidential discussion
  • Goal Alignment: Understanding your investment objectives
  • Q&A Session: Address all your concerns and questions
  • Structure Explanation: Detailed review of terms and process
Outcome:

Clear understanding of whether our partnership aligns with your goals. No obligation at this stage.

02

Documentation & Review

Days 4-7

We prepare and send your personalized Loan Agreement for review with your legal/financial advisor.

What to Expect:

  • Personalized Agreement: Customized for your investment amount
  • Legal Review: We encourage review with your advisor
  • Clarification Calls: Additional calls if needed for specific clauses
  • Final Review: Ensure all terms are understood
Documents You Need:
PAN Card
Aadhaar Card
Cancelled Cheque
Passport Photo
03

Agreement Execution

Day 8

Sign and return the executed agreement along with KYC documents. We countersign and return the fully executed copy.

What to Expect:

  • Signing: Execute the agreement (digital/physical)
  • Return Documents: Send signed agreement + KYC documents
  • Countersignature: We countersign and return executed copy
  • Security Setup: Post-dated cheques prepared as security
Agreement Highlights:
Payment Schedule
Security Terms
Dispute Resolution
Obligations
04

Funding & Activation

Day 9

Transfer funds via secure bank transfer. We acknowledge receipt and activate your partnership.

What to Expect:

  • Fund Transfer: Via NEFT/RTGS to designated account
  • Confirmation: Send transaction confirmation email
  • Receipt Acknowledgment: We confirm receipt within 24 hours
  • Verification: Funds verified and partnership activated
Funding Information:
Processing Time 1-2 hours (NEFT/RTGS)
Minimum Investment ‚Çπ25,000
Bank Details Provided in agreement
05

Onboarding & First Payment

Days 10-45

Receive welcome kit, payment schedule, and first monthly return within the payment cycle.

What to Expect:

  • Welcome Kit: Email with all partnership documents
  • Payment Schedule: Detailed amortization schedule
  • Designated Contact: Your primary point of contact
  • First Payment: By 10th working day of next month
Welcome Kit Includes:
Executed Agreement Copy
Payment Dates Calendar
Portal Login (if applicable)
Contact Information

Fast Track Option

For experienced investors who have already reviewed the sample agreement, the process can be completed in 3-5 working days from consultation to funding.

Standard Timeline

For first-time investors or those requiring detailed review, the typical timeline is 7-10 working days from initial contact to partnership activation.

Important Notes for Onboarding

Key information for a smooth partnership beginning

Legal Advice Recommended

We strongly encourage reviewing the agreement with your legal/financial advisor before signing.

Source of Funds

Ensure funds are from lawful sources and you can provide verification if required.

No Cooling-off Period

Once agreement is executed and funds transferred, the partnership is binding.

Payment Cycle

First payment is made in the next payment cycle after funding (by 10th working day).
